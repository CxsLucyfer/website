Customize everything

Change the web to your needs. Change the background color, fonts, text color, font sizes, remove images and more. You name it, with Helperbird you can change everything on the page.

Dyslexia Support

Helperbird has over 14 fonts designed to help with reading on the web, from Lexend to OpenDyslexic, you name it. Overlays, Reading Guides, Reading masks, Text to Speech, and Dyslexia rulers. Plus many more features.

Accessibility tools

Helperbird is designed on the word accessibility, change everything from the cursor size, shortcuts, Immersive Reader, Reading Mode (Remove all distractions), and our powerful OCR tool. Plus many more features.

Increase Productivity

From annotate any website you visit, immersive reader, Voice typing, Reading Mode, removing ads and 20 other features. Helperbird users make getting work done quicker and easier on the web.

View clutter-free web pages

Helperbird gives you a Reading Mode that removes ads and distractions from any webpage with complete customization.

Annotate any website

Highlight or annotate web pages, documents, PDFs, ebooks and more to improve research and study. Leaving sticking notes that help you with your research and homework.
