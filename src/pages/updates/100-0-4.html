Release notes for Helperbird 100.0.3

This update is all about the nice touches and fixes that make Helperbird even better.

Whats new?

  • Update: Massively improved word prediction for Google Docs.
  • New: We added 21 new voices (including natural) with support for 15 languages.
  • Fix: Darkmode support for our iOS app.
  • New: Added a share button in the Reader Mode. Quickly and easily share what your reading to Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin.
  • Update: Added country flags next to each voice so you know what's it designed for. .
