Release notes for Helperbird 100.1.1

This update is all about the nice touches and fixes that make Helperbird even better.

What's new?

📝 Sticky notes got redesigned

We have redesigned the sticky notes to make them more intuitive and easy to use. All buttons are now on the same line, and the sticky note is now more accessible. They also include word and icons.

🦊 Firefox support

Helperbird now supports the latest version of Firefox.

💬 Helperbird apps now in the quick action

All Helperbird apps are now in the quick action menu. Check out the pink icons.

🎫 Onboarding Tour

All new users or even current users can get a quick tour of Helperbird. Check under the settings cog.

🎤 Text to speech voices are free

All current voices are free for the next 60 days. We might extend this based on feedback.

🖼️ Helperbird apps get redesigned

The Helperbird apps have been redesigned to make them more intuitive and easy to use. No more hiding the search bar and better use of the keyboard.

🖼️ Show image title & Alts on hover

Helperbird now show alt and title attributes on images when you hover on them.

🌶️ Dropdown menu support keyboard navigation

All dropdown menus now support keyboard navigation.

Dropdown menu support keyboard navigation

All dropdown menus now support keyboard navigation.

📙 Reader mode improvements

We can now change the themes to suit your style. You can also get more content than before.

📙 Immersive reader upgraded to 1.2.0

We have upgraded the Immersive reader to version 1.2.0, bringing more features like translation support.
