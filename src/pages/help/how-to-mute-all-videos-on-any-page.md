## What is Mute videos?

The Mute videos feature adds a gradient of colors to the text on any website, pdf or app.

## How to Mute videos

- Open Helperbird on Chrome, Firefox or Edge
- Scroll to the **Mute** card.
- Toggle the on/off switch to **On**.
- Thats it.
