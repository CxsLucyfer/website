## How to change the cursor size

- Open Helperbird on Chrome, Firefox or Edge
- Scroll to the **Cursor size** card.
- Toggle the on/off switch to **On**.
- You can also change the size from small to large with the slider.
- Thats it.

## Supported sizes

- Extra large
- Large
- Medium
- Small
