## How to highlight paragraphs

- Open Helperbird on Chrome, Firefox or Edge
- Scroll to the **Highlight paragraphs** card.
- Toggle the on/off switch to **On**.
- Thats it.

## Features

- Highlight paragraphs color.
- Highlight paragraphs width.
